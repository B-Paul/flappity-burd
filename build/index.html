Flappy Trump was designed and created by Paul Anderson

The game was built using Babel for ES6 to ES5 conversion, Gulp, Browserify, and BrowserSync for builds, and SASS for CSS.

The font used is VT323.

Donald Trump sound bites and the image for the Trump head were sourced from news reporting and are believed to be a fair use.

Additional sounds were provided by Benboncan, under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 license, and by qubodup and Ekokubza123, through public domain contributions.
